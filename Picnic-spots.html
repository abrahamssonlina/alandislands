When to visit
The climate has its peak during July and is considered to be the high season. Many of the island's events also take place during this month.
How to get here
There are numerous ferries and cruise ships in traffic between Stockholm, Helsinki, Turku and the Åland Islands, eg. Viking Line, Eckerölinjen and Birka Cruises.
